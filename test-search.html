How to Test

  1. Open the app at http://localhost:3000/Melodyx/
  2. Login with Spotify Premium account
  3. Navigate to the player page
  4. Click the search button (magnifying glass icon) in the header
  5. Or use keyboard shortcut Ctrl+K (Windows) or Cmd+K (Mac)
  6. Test search functionality:
    • Type to search for tracks, artists, albums, or playlists
    • Switch between category tabs
    • Click "My Playlists" to see your playlists
    • Click "Liked" to see your liked tracks
    • Play results directly from search
    • Add tracks to queue
